The story begins, in a garden...

The afternoon sun was so warm it made Alice drowsy, and she couldn't help yawning at the dull textbook. Just then, a white rabbit in a suit, clutching a pocket watch, dashed past her. “Oh dear! I'm going to be late!” it muttered anxiously. Alice snapped back to attention, but the rabbit was already far away. Only those words lingered in her ears—was this all a fantasy, or had she truly encountered something weird?
