What did the cake do?...

After eating that small piece of cake, a strange wave of heat instantly surged through her entire body. Alice realized with horror that she was rapidly growing taller, her shoulders bumping against the low ceiling, her knees forced to curl up in discomfort. She felt as though she'd been stuffed into a box far too small, unable to move.The corridor, which had seemed spacious moments before, now felt unbearably cramped. The small door leading to the wondrous garden appeared to her eyes as tiny as a mouse hole, distant and unreachable.
