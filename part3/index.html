In a dark cave...

Alice followed the white rabbit closely and plunged headlong into the darkness. When she awoke, she found herself in a long, dimly lit hall lined with many small doors, all tightly shut. In the center of the hallway stood a table. Upon it sat a small bottle labeled “Drink Me” and a piece of cake resting on a porcelain plate, accompanied by a neat label reading “Eat Me.” A strange silence hung in the air, as if the entire hall were holding its breath, waiting to see what she would do next.
